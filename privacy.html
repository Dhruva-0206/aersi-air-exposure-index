What AERSI is, how the data behind it should be interpreted, what (if anything) is collected about visitors, and how the code and dataset are licensed.

What AERSI Is

AERSI (Air Exposure Severity Index) is an independent research index providing informational air quality analysis. It is not medical advice. Scores are derived from CPCB monitoring data which may contain gaps, sensor errors, or delays. Do not make health or safety decisions based solely on AERSI scores. Always refer to official government advisories for health guidance.

Data Disclaimer

Air quality data is sourced from the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) via data.gov.in. The AERSI pipeline has documented data gaps (105 missing days between January and May 2026 due to API infrastructure issues). Scores reflect the most recent available 30-day window and may not reflect current conditions if the pipeline has not run recently. AERSI scores are provided as-is with no warranty of accuracy or completeness.

Privacy Notice

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data directly. No cookies, analytics, login, or user tracking of any kind. However, the following third-party services process visitor data as part of serving this website:

  • Vercel (hosting) — logs request metadata including IP addresses
  • Cloudflare cdnjs — serves Chart.js; may log visitor IPs
  • CARTO tile servers — serves the interactive map tiles; sees every map pan and zoom including IP address
  • jsDelivr — serves mapping libraries; may log visitor IPs

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 and GDPR for EU visitors, IP addresses may qualify as personal data. These services have their own privacy policies. If you prefer not to have your IP logged by tile servers, you may disable JavaScript.
